Cartography | Programming | Geospatial

Brady has been engaged in the arts/sciences of GIS, Cartography, and Programming since 2014. Whether he is working on a geospatial trail network for the Bureau of Land Management, creating the back end framework for an engineering firm's telemeter webmap, or making a topographic map of Mars' Olympus Mons, he strives to achieve high levels of techical accuracy, usability, and aesthetic appeal.
A graduate of Humboldt State University with a degree in Environmental Science with Emphasis in Geospatial Informations Systems, Brady currently resides in Eureka, California.
